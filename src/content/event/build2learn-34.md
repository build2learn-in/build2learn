## About the Event

Welcome to build2learn - Where Innovation Meets Community!

> Start the new year by transforming your ideas into meaningful, real-world applications.

Bring in your ideas, skills and a open mind to learn. This hackathon gives you an opportunity to learn and build with others, discuss on the ideas others build on.

Make mistakes, troubleshoot, ideate and build upon your skills 🛠

### Who Should Join?

- **Aspiring Builders**: Have a side project you've been dreaming of? This is your chance!
- **Students**: Looking for mentorship and guidance? We've got you covered!
- **Mentors**: Want to help others reach their potential? Join us!

<br />

## Instructions

1. Come prepared with your project idea/ or don't worry you can join with others.
2. Bring your own laptop
3. Network and collaborate with fellow builders

<br />

## Hackathon Rules

1. Projects must be built during the event
2. Teams can be 1-3 people
3. Final presentations will be 5 minutes per team
